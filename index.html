Projects & Organisations

Project Manager of Prefragrance 2017-2019

Prefragrance is an online platform designed to meet the needs of fragrance enthusiasts in Korea. This service provides a platform for users to share their preferred fragrances and write reviews about them, which helps customers make informed decisions when purchasing fragrance products online. The platform also functions as a social media platform where users can follow each other and discover new scents based on similar fragrance preferences.

With machine learning algorithms, Prefragrance can learn from user reviews and experiences to recommend fragrances that are likely to suit the user's taste. The innovative use of machine learning technology enables the service to personalize its recommendations to each user, providing a unique and tailored experience.

Prefragrance offers a user-friendly and informative approach to exploring fragrances, connecting with other fragrance enthusiasts, and making informed purchasing decisions.

As the project manager, I was responsible for overseeing the entire project and worked closely with the front-end and back-end teams to build the logic for the website and develop the customer experience, building on my original idea for the service. My role included identifying and developing the concept for the service, managing the project timelines and resources, and ensuring that the platform met the needs and preferences of the target audience.
